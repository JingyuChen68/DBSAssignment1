Thoughtful Design

TV Mode

Dock your Switch and transform any television into your gaming space. The console scales seamlessly, delivering HD visuals on larger displays.

Perfect for immersive single-player adventures or multiplayer sessions with friends and family.

Handheld Gaming

The freedom to play untethered. With a 6.3-inch screen and intuitive controls, gaming follows you wherever life takes you.

Commutes, travels, or simply moving between rooms — your adventure never has to stop.

Tabletop Experience

A middle path. Set the console down, detach the Joy-Cons, and share the moment with others or play solo on a propped screen.

Intimate gaming in shared spaces, without the isolation of handheld or the setup of TV mode.

Display & Graphics

6.2-inch touchscreen with 720p HD resolution. NVIDIA Tegra processor delivers smooth, responsive gameplay.

Performance & Speed

Custom hardware engineered for 3 distinct play modes. Seamless transitions between TV, handheld, and tabletop.

Battery Life

Up to 6.5 hours of continuous handheld play on a single charge. Extended battery models available.

Connectivity

WiFi, Bluetooth, online multiplayer. Play solo or connect with millions of players worldwide.
