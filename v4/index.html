Nintendo Switch

The Future of Portable Gaming

The Console That Evolves With You

Nintendo Switch redefines what a gaming system can be. Seamlessly transition between handheld, tabletop, and TV modes with a single device. Whether you're on your couch or across the world, your games move with you.

"Play Your Way" — Three modes. Infinite possibilities.

TV Mode

Dock your Switch and experience your favorite games on the big screen. With the included stand, you get a traditional home console experience without compromise. Perfect for family game nights or solo adventures.

Tabletop Mode

Stand your Switch up and share the Joy-Con controllers. Perfect for gaming with friends on the go. The flexible kickstand means stable gameplay anywhere — from hotel rooms to coffee shops. Social gaming has never been more portable.

Handheld Mode

The ultimate portable gaming experience. Play anywhere with up to 6.5 hours of battery life. The integrated Joy-Con controllers attach directly to the sides of the console, making it compact and ready for adventure.
