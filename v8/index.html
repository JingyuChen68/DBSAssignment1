Three Play Modes

TV Mode

Dock your console and experience gaming on the big screen. Includes Joy-Con grip for extended comfort and traditional gamepad-style play.

  • Full HD 1920×1080 output
  • Connected to power for extended sessions
  • Supports up to 4 Joy-Con wireless controllers
  • Perfect for multiplayer gaming with friends

Handheld Mode

Attach Joy-Cons to the sides and take your games anywhere. Play on the go with the portable 6.2-inch display and built-in speakers.

  • 6.2-inch LCD capacitive touchscreen
  • 5.5 to 9 hours battery life
  • Built-in microphone and speakers
  • USB-C for rapid charging

Tabletop Mode

Use the adjustable stand to prop up your console. Share your screen with others and play together—perfect for parties and social gaming.

  • Durable built-in kickstand
  • Detach Joy-Cons for individual play
  • Shared screen experience
  • Ideal for local multiplayer games
