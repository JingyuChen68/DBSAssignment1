Experience Three Ways

📺 TV Mode

Dock your console and transform any television into your gaming hub. Perfect for multiplayer sessions, immersive adventures, and family gaming.

✋ Handheld Mode

Detach the Joy-Cons and take gaming anywhere. Full-featured gaming in your hands, wherever you go — no compromise, no limits.

🎮 Tabletop Mode

Stand it up and play. Share the screen with a friend or enjoy solo gaming with the console propped up on any flat surface.

TV Mode Gaming

Experience the full power of your console on a larger display. Stunning visuals, immersive sound, and responsive controls make TV mode perfect for single-player campaigns or competitive multiplayer.

From Nintendo exclusives to third-party AAA titles, enjoy thousands of games in beautiful HD.

Handheld Gaming

Pure portability. The Nintendo Switch is engineered for gaming on the go. Your library travels with you, whether you're commuting, traveling, or just moving between rooms.

Intuitive controls and a vibrant 6.2-inch screen deliver the full console experience in handheld form.

Tabletop Experience

The middle ground. Set your console on any surface, detach the Joy-Cons, and enjoy gaming without docking or holding. Perfect for sharing moments with friends or gaming in unconventional spaces.

Flexible, social, and always ready to adapt to your environment.

Processor & Power

Custom NVIDIA Tegra X1 chipset. Engineered for smooth, responsive gameplay across all three play modes.

Display Technology

6.2-inch LCD touchscreen with 720p HD resolution. Vibrant colors and responsive input for seamless control.

Storage & Expandability

32GB internal storage with microSD card support. Expand your library to thousands of games without limits.

Connectivity

WiFi 5GHz, Bluetooth 4.1, USB-C. Play online with millions, or enjoy local multiplayer with friends.
