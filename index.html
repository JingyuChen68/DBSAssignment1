MPCS 51238 — Assignment 1

Nintendo Switch
Landing Page Exploration

25 iterations exploring layouts, moods, and visual styles for a Nintendo Switch product page. From wild experimentation to refined convergence.

This project starts wide — radically different aesthetics, tones, and audiences — then narrows as patterns emerge. Versions 1–10 explore wildly different directions. Versions 11–15 start mixing the best elements. Versions 16–19 converge on a dark editorial gaming aesthetic. Versions 20–25 refine small details until v25 feels just right.

Final Pick — Version 25

After exploring 10 wildly different directions, mixing the best elements, converging on a dark editorial gaming aesthetic, and refining typography, spacing, and interactions — v25 brings it all together. The dark background lets the content breathe, Playfair Display adds editorial sophistication, and the Nintendo red keeps it unmistakably gaming.
